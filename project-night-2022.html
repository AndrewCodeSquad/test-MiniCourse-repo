CodeSquad Project Night

2022 Project Night

Tuesday, June 7, 2022
6:30pm-8:30pm

At this event, 16 graduates from our latest cohort will present the web applications they've built to an audience of software execs, recruiters, friends and supporters.

Are you looking to hire software developers?
Are you hoping to diversify your workforce?
Are you a prospective student or volunteer?

If so, please join us! This is a free event, to be held online. Using our conference software, guests can move freely about the event, clicking to join individual "tables" where each student will present their work and take questions.

Energize your workforce with CodeSquad graduates

CodeSquad graduates have built their apps using web technologies like Node, Express, Mongo and Heroku. Our grads have become Software Engineers, Application Developers and more at companies including:

Apps to track wildlife, find an apartment, and practice transformative justice

Our students have built apps to help find an apartment using data visualization; find a low-stress bathroom if you're gender-nonconforming, organize volunteers for park cleanups, and track the shelf life of groceries.
